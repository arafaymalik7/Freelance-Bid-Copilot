# Manual Test Scenarios

1. Run the full pipeline with a detailed restaurant website brief covering pages, menu, reservations, and launch target.
2. Run the pipeline with a vague mobile app brief that includes no budget or timeline.
3. Test a content writing brief with a tight deadline, such as 10 SEO articles in 2 weeks.
4. Test a UI/UX brief with conflicting design preferences, such as "modern and traditional."
5. Test a brief that mentions a very high budget, such as `$50,000`, and verify pricing notes stay sensible.
6. Test an informal brief with typos and casual phrasing to confirm the pipeline still extracts usable structure.
7. Complete two refinement rounds before generating the proposal and confirm the round counter and updated questions behave correctly.
8. Skip refinement entirely and continue straight to proposal generation.
9. Copy the proposal draft, subject line, and client reply using the UI copy buttons.
10. Click Start Over from the results view and confirm all state resets to the initial brief screen.
